Expression Karaoke

Facial expressions can be tricky! Let’s practice some with this tool. It helps you see and improve your non-verbal skills, based on science, to connect with patients.
We’re not giving you a formula. We understand that each patient situation is different.



Compassionate expression

A compassionate facial expression can make a patient feel understood, supported and valued.


Welcoming expression

A welcoming facial expression can help establish rapport, trust and comfort with the patient.


Listening expression

A neutral, listening facial expression can help the doctor maintain objectivity and respect for the patient.
